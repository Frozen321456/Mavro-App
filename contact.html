How long does shipping take?

Shipping typically takes 7-12 business days for China to Bangladesh delivery. Express shipping options are available at checkout.

We offer flexible MOQs starting from as low as 10 pieces per style for our wholesale clients. Contact us for specific requirements.

Do you offer custom orders?

Yes! We can accommodate custom orders and private labeling for bulk purchases. Please contact our wholesale team for details.
