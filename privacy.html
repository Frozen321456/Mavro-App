Our Commitment to Privacy

At Espera Mavro, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our global sourcing and wholesale platform. We are committed to ensuring your data is handled securely and transparently.

Our Promise: We never sell your personal information. We only share necessary data with trusted partners to fulfill your orders, and always in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

By accessing or using Espera Mavro, you consent to the practices described in this policy. If you do not agree, please refrain from using our services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Account Information

When you create an account with Espera Mavro, we collect:

  • Full name – for order processing and shipping
  • Email address – for account communication and order updates
  • Phone number – for shipping coordination and support
  • Password – securely encrypted for account access
  • Billing details – for payment processing

1.2 Shipping Information

To deliver your orders, we collect:

  • Shipping address (street, city, postal code, country)
  • Recipient phone number
  • Preferred delivery time (if provided)

1.3 Order Information

When you place an order through Espera Mavro, we collect:

  • Products purchased and quantities
  • Price and order value
  • Shipping preferences
  • Special instructions or notes

1.4 Technical Data

When you visit our website, we automatically collect:

  • IP address and location data
  • Browser type and device information
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Cookie data and preferences

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us provide and improve our services:

Order Processing: To process, fulfill, and ship your orders through our global sourcing network
Account Management: To create and maintain your account, provide customer support
Communication: To send order updates, shipping notifications, and respond to inquiries
Improvement: To analyze usage patterns and enhance our platform features
Security: To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and maintain platform integrity
Important: To deliver your orders, we share necessary shipping information with our global fulfillment partners who handle logistics and delivery. These partners are contractually bound to protect your data and use it only for order fulfillment.

3. Information Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We only share data in these specific circumstances:

3.1 Fulfillment Partners

We work with trusted global logistics and fulfillment partners to deliver your orders. These partners receive:

  • Name and contact details
  • Shipping address
  • Order contents
  • Tracking information

Our partners are contractually required to use this information solely for order fulfillment and cannot use it for any other purpose.

3.2 Payment Processors

Payment transactions are handled by secure third-party payment processors. We do not store your complete payment card details. Payment processors are PCI-DSS compliant and follow strict security standards.

3.3 Legal Compliance

We may disclose information if required by law, regulation, or valid legal process (such as a court order or subpoena).

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction. You will be notified of any such change.

Note: We only share the minimum information necessary to fulfill your orders and provide our services.

4. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your browser and our servers is encrypted using SSL/TLS technology
  • Access Control: Strict access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access personal data
  • Secure Storage: Data is stored in secure, monitored servers with firewall protection
  • Regular Audits: We regularly review and update our security practices

While we take every precaution, no method of internet transmission is 100% secure. We strive to protect your data but cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights:

Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
Right to Deletion: Request deletion of your data
Right to Restrict: Limit how we use your data
Right to Portability: Receive your data in a portable format
Right to Object: Object to certain data processing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below. We will respond within 30 days.

6. Cookies & Tracking

Espera Mavro uses cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Remember your login and preferences
  • Understand how you use our site
  • Improve site performance and user experience
  • Provide relevant product recommendations

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences. Disabling cookies may affect certain site functionality.

7. Children's Privacy

Espera Mavro is not intended for users under 18. We do not knowingly collect information from minors. If you believe a minor has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

8. International Data Transfers

As a global sourcing platform, your information may be transferred and processed in countries where our fulfillment partners operate. These countries may have different data protection laws. By using Espera Mavro, you consent to such transfers.

We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place through contractual agreements with our partners.

9. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date. Significant changes will be notified via email or site notice. Continued use of Espera Mavro constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
