1. General Refund Policy

Espera Mavro acts as a technology platform connecting you with global suppliers. Therefore:

Important: Refunds always follow the policies of the original supplier/seller. We process refunds only after supplier approval and are not responsible for supplier refund delays or rejections.

1.1 Refund Timeline

  • Request Processing: 2-3 business days for initial review
  • Supplier Response: 3-7 business days (varies by supplier)
  • Refund Processing: 5-10 business days after supplier approval
  • Total Estimated Time: 10-20 business days

1.2 Refund Methods

  • Original Payment Method: Refunds are issued to the original payment method whenever possible
  • Store Credit: May be offered as an alternative (with 5% bonus credit)
  • Bank Transfer: For specific cases where original payment can't be refunded

1.3 Deductions

The following are always non-refundable:

  • Shipping and delivery charges
  • Payment gateway fees (typically 2-3%)
  • Customs duties and import taxes
  • Service and handling fees
  • Insurance charges (if applicable)

3. Product Category Guidelines

3.1 Electronics & Gadgets

  • Warranty: Manufacturer warranty applies where available
  • Defects: 7-day defect reporting window
  • Compatibility: Buyer responsible for checking specifications (voltage, plugs, etc.)
  • Returns: No returns for opened electronics unless defective

3.2 Fashion & Apparel

  • Sizing: Size charts provided - buyer responsible for choosing correct size
  • Color: Slight color variations due to monitor settings are normal
  • Returns: No returns for size issues (except supplier error)

3.3 Fragile Items (Glassware, Ceramics)

  • Risk: Ordered at buyer's own risk
  • Packaging: Suppliers use professional packaging, but damage may occur
  • Claims: Must provide unboxing video showing damage
  • Refund: If approved, maximum 50% of product value

3.4 Cosmetics & Beauty

  • Expiry: Must check expiration dates upon arrival
  • Allergies: Buyer responsible for checking ingredients
  • Regulations: Must comply with local cosmetic regulations
  • Returns: No returns for opened products

3.5 Fishing & Outdoor

  • Quality: Inspect immediately upon delivery
  • Claims: 48-hour window for defect reporting
  • Usage: No refunds for wear and tear

5. How to Request a Refund

Step 1: Submit Request

Email esperamavro@gmail.com with your order number, photos/videos, and detailed description of the issue. Must be within 48 hours of delivery.

Step 2: Review (2-3 Days)

We review your request and verify evidence. We may request additional information or documentation.

Step 3: Supplier Communication (3-7 Days)

We contact the supplier with your evidence. Supplier decides whether to approve refund and amount.

Step 4: Resolution (5-10 Days)

If approved, refund is processed. You'll receive confirmation email with details. Store credit (if chosen) is added immediately.

Pro Tip: Always film an unboxing video showing the package from all angles before opening. This is the strongest evidence for damage claims.

6. Shipping-Related Issues

6.1 Delayed Shipments

Delivery estimates are estimates only. We cannot refund for delays because:

  • International shipping involves customs clearance, weather, and carrier delays
  • We provide tracking information but cannot control carrier performance
  • Some countries have slower postal systems

6.2 Lost Shipments

  • Domestic (Bangladesh): Investigate with local carrier first. If confirmed lost, partial refund may be considered after 15 days.
  • International: Must wait 45 days from shipping date. File claim with carrier. Refund depends on carrier investigation.

6.3 Customs & Duties

  • Buyer is responsible for all customs duties, taxes, and import fees
  • If shipment is seized by customs, no refund will be issued
  • If buyer refuses to pay duties, shipment is abandoned - no refund
Permits Required: Certain products (cosmetics, electronics, medicines) may require government permits. You must obtain these before ordering. No refunds if shipment is held for missing permits.

7. Special Circumstances

7.1 Force Majeure

In cases of natural disasters, pandemics, wars, or government actions:

  • Refund eligibility depends solely on supplier's decision
  • If supplier denies refund, we are not liable
  • We will assist in communication but cannot guarantee outcomes

7.2 Supplier Bankruptcy

If a supplier goes out of business before fulfilling orders:

  • We will attempt to recover funds but cannot guarantee
  • No refund obligation from Espera Mavro
  • Consider purchasing shipping insurance for high-value orders

7.3 Partial Shipments

If your order arrives in multiple packages:

  • Each package is tracked separately
  • Missing items: Wait 15 days after last tracking update
  • Partial refund may be available for truly missing items
